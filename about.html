Roksolana 'Roxy' Boiko

Hi. I'm passionate, coffee addict web/graphic designer and developer. I grew up in Ukraine and graduaded with a Bachelor's degree in graphic design from University of Information Technology and Management in Rzeszów, Poland.

I started out life in print, specialising in branding, photo-retouching, artworking and packaging design. Over the years I have moved into motion, 3D visualisation, app's and web, but my core values are still the same – it doesn't matter what format the end product is, I still aim to provide insight, experience and creative knowledge.

One of my greatest assets is taking any problem and working out the best and most effective ways to approach it. I believe this is one of the reasons I have such a diverse skill set, using different techniques to get the job done.

If you have cooperation offer feel free to contact me.

I'm a graphic/ web designer and developer based in Lviv, Ukraine.
